About Me

I am a coffee addicted, workaholic, who spends most of my free time hanging out with my dog Sephie. We spend our days lounging outside by the pool and chasing lizards. I am originally from Orlando, but spent the last 3 years in Colorado. While I had an amazing time and many wild adventures in the Rocky Mountains, I realized soon after moving that the Sunshine State and its beautiful beaches is where I belonged. Since being home, I've made it a personal goal to follow what interests me and learn many new skills which include paddleboarding, kickboxing, and off-shore fishing. I am excited to now tackle a new craft and become an expert Web Developer!
